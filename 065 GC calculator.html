065 calculation method:
Methane fraction is calculated as Cg = Concent_CH4 (%) / 100.
Methane partial pressure is calculated as Pg = Cg × Total_P.
Dissolved methane is calculated as C_A = (Molar_water × Pg × Molecular_mass_CH4 × 1000) / Kh.
Headspace methane is calculated as C_AH = (Cg × Molecular_mass_CH4 × 1000 / Standard volume at NTP) × (V_headspace / V_sludge) × (T_reference / T_sample_K).
Total methane is calculated as TC = C_A + C_AH.

  • Sample_name - Sample identifier.
  • Sampling Technique - e.g. HS in field, prefilled NaOH + sludge.
  • Date_GC - GC analysis date.
  • Lab_GC - Lab/location where GC was run.
  • Concent_CH4 (%) - Methane concentration in the headspace from GC (%).
  • V_vial (mL) - Total vial volume.
  • V_headspace (mL) - Headspace volume in mL.
  • V_poison (mL) - Poison/reagent volume, e.g. NaOH.
  • V_sludge (mL) - Sludge volume in mL.
  • Cg (vol CH4/vol sample) - Methane fraction from GC %, auto-calculated if missing.
  • Total_P (atm) - Total pressure in atm.
  • Pg (atm CH4) - Methane partial pressure, auto-calculated if missing.
  • Molar_water (mol/L) - Usually 55.5 mol/L.
  • T_reference (K) - Usually 273.15 K.
  • T_sample (°C) - Sample temperature in Celsius.
  • T_sample (K) - Sample temperature in Kelvin, auto-calculated if missing.
  • Kh (atm/molfraction) - Henry constant used for methane.
  • Molecular_mass_CH4 - Usually 16.04 g/mol.
  • Standard volume at NTP (L/mol) - Usually 22.4 L/mol.

Auto-calculated outputs: Cg, Pg, T_sample (K), C_A, C_AH, TC.
