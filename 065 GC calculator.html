GC Version 2 calculation method:
Liquid added to vial = (Wf - Wi) / Density
Sludge volume = ((Wf - Wi) / Density) - Brine_vol - V_poison
Headspace volume = V_vial - ((Wf - Wi) / Density)
Methane fraction = Cg = Concent_CH4 (%) / 100
Methane partial pressure = Pg = Cg × Total_P
Dissolved methane = C_A = (Molar_water × Pg × Molecular_mass_CH4 × 1000) / Kh
Headspace methane = C_AH = (Cg × Molecular_mass_CH4 × 1000 / Standard volume at NTP) × (V_headspace / V_sludge) × (T_reference / T_sample_K)
Total methane = TC = C_A + C_AH

Expected input columns:

  • Sampling date - Sampling date.
  • Date_GC - GC analysis date.
  • Sample_name - Sample identifier.
  • Sampling Technique - Sampling method.
  • Lab_GC - Lab/location where GC was run.
  • Concent_CH4 (%) - Methane concentration from GC.
  • Wi (g) - Empty vial and cap weight.
  • Wf (g) - Vial, cap, and liquid weight.
  • Density (g/ml) - Sludge/sample density. If blank, calculator assumes 1 g/mL.
  • Brine_vol (mL) - Brine volume added.
  • V_vial (mL) - Total vial volume.
  • V_poison (mL) - Poison/reagent volume added.

Calculated columns:

  • V_headspace (mL)
  • V_sludge (mL)
  • Cg (vol CH4/vol sample)
  • Total_P (atm)
  • Pg (atm CH4)
  • Molar_water (mol/L)
  • T_reference (K)
  • T_sample (°C)
  • T_sample (K)
  • Kh (atm/molfraction)
  • Molecular_mass_CH4 (g/mol)
  • Standard volume at NTP (L/mol)
  • C_A (mg/L)
  • C_AH (mg/L)
  • TC (mg/L)
