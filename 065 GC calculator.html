065 calculation method:
Headspace methane is calculated using the Ideal Gas Law: n = PV / RT, then converted from moles to mg and divided by sludge volume.
Liquid methane is calculated using the Bunsen coefficient, partial pressure of methane, sludge volume, and methane molecular weight.
Total methane is calculated as: TC_065 = C_CH4 hs + C_CH4 liq.

  • Sample_name - Sample identifier.
  • Sampling Technique - e.g. HS in field, prefilled NaOH + sludge.
  • Date_GC - GC analysis date.
  • Lab_GC - Lab/location where GC was run.
  • Concent_CH4 (%) - Methane concentration in headspace from GC (%).
  • V_vial (mL) - Total vial volume.
  • Density (g/mL) - Density of sample/sludge.
  • V_headspace (mL) - Headspace volume in mL.
  • V_poison (mL) - Poison/reagent volume, e.g. NaOH.
  • V_sludge (mL) - Sludge volume in mL.
  • Total_P (atm) - Total pressure in atm.
  • R (L atm/K mol) - Gas constant, normally 0.082.
  • T (°C) - Temperature in Celsius.
  • MW (g/mol) - Methane molecular weight, normally 16.04.
  • Bunsen 21°C - Bunsen coefficient for methane at 21°C, e.g. 0.0336.

Auto-calculated outputs: P_CH4_hs, n_CH4_hs, m_CH4_hs, C_CH4_hs, n_CH4_liq, m_CH4_liq, C_CH4_liq, TC_065.
