Column Definitions:
  • Site - Cotton Valley (CV), Shieldhall (SH), Laighpark (LP), Philipshill (PH)
  • reference - 1,2,3: the number of replicates, c: control sample
  • reference_N - Flexible number of reference categories (reference_1, reference_2, reference_3, ..., reference_n, etc.).
    Explaination of the referece are provide in the lab note
  • Wi - estimate weight of the empty vial and cap (g)
  • Wf - weight of the vial, cap, and liquid samples (g)
  • Density - density of the liquid sample (g/ml)
  • V_bott - Bottle volume (mL)
  • V_hs - Headspace volume (mL)
  • V_expansion - Expansion volume (mL) - Optional, if applicable
  • T - Temperature (°C)
  • P - Pressure (atm)
  • Cgv - Methane concentration in the headspace (%)
  • CO2% - Carbon Dioxide concentration in the headspace (%)
  • Cal - Calculated Methane in liquid phase (mg/L) (Auto-calculated)
  • Cah - Calculated Methane in headspace (mg/L) (Auto-calculated)
  • TC - Total Methane Content (Cal + Cah) (mg/L) (Auto-calculated)
  • Cal_CO2 - Calculated CO2 in liquid phase (mg/L) (Auto-calculated)
  • Cah_CO2 - Calculated CO2 in headspace (mg/L) (Auto-calculated)
  • TC_CO2 - Total CO2 Content (Cal_CO2 + Cah_CO2) (mg/L) (Auto-calculated)
  • poison - NaOH, NaCl, mix: type of poison used in the experiment
  • vortex - yes, no, maybe: indicates if vortex was applied
  • sonication - yes, no, maybe: indicates if sonication was applied
  • brine - yes, no, maybe: indicates if brine was used
  • heat - yes, no, maybe: indicates if heat was applied
